IoT report diagram style (use verbatim in prompts)

Medium: flat vector diagram (not photo), crisp UI/architecture schematic.
Rendering: simple rounded rectangles and thin connector lines; 2px stroke; no gradients; no shadows; no 3D.
Palette: match report CSS — background #0d0d0d, surface #1a1a1a, border #2a2a2a, text #e5e5e5, secondary text #888888, accent #6b9fff.
Typography: clean sans-serif; all caps for small labels where appropriate.
Detail level: low-to-medium; emphasize data flow and system boundaries.
Export: high contrast; leave generous padding; no decorative elements.
