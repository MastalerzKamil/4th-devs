Operational risks (what to monitor)

  • Backpressure propagation: Kafka lag → consumer delay → alerting noise.
  • Credential churn: device cert rotation failures manifest as auth spikes on ingest API.
  • Hot partitions: poor partition keys cause uneven load and artificial latency.
  • Silent drops: parsing failures without DLQ/metrics are the highest-severity failure mode.
